reload_delay long The time needed to put a single ammo into magazines.
The reloading time of a magazine (or a gun with magazine attached) is
<reload_delay> * <remaining ammo> (ticks)
or <reload_delay> * <remaining ammo> / 20 (seconds)
skin ItemSkin not-null Set the item's skin
